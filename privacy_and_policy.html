Privacy Policy — Word Guess Game Last Updated: March 30, 2026 Word Guess Game values your privacy. While most data is stored locally, we use industry-standard third-party services for advertising, app analytics, and in-app purchases to improve your experience. 1. Overview Word Guess Game is an entertaining word puzzle app designed to test your vocabulary. This policy describes how we handle data through our app and third-party integrations. 2. Data Collection & Local Storage Your game progress, scores, and statistics never leave your device. All game history, high scores, and settings are stored in your device's local database and preferences. We do not collect personal information like names, emails, or photos, and we do not have access to your private gameplay data. 3. Third-Party Services To support the development and improve the app, we integrate the following third-party services which may collect certain information (such as Advertising IDs and device usage data): Google AdMob: Displaying banner ads (Privacy Policy) Unity Ads: Displaying banner ads (Privacy Policy) Firebase Analytics: App usage statistics (Privacy Policy) Google Play Billing: Payment processing for Premium Features (Privacy Policy) 4. Advertising and Analytics We use AdMob and Unity Ads to show advertisements. These providers may use your device's Advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can opt-out of personalized ads via your Android device settings. We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app (e.g., game starts, guesses, and button clicks). This data is anonymized and used solely for improving the app's functionality and user experience. 5. Permissions Internet/Network State: Required for ad delivery, analytics reporting, and verifying premium purchases. Advertising ID: Required by Google Play services for analytics and showing relevant ads. 6. Data Deletion You can delete all locally stored game data and statistics by uninstalling the app or clearing its storage in your device's settings. 7. Children's Privacy Our app is designed for a general audience. While the game relies on vocabulary, children can play it safely. Advertisements are served in accordance with the policies of our ad networks (AdMob & Unity Ads), which require compliance with children's privacy regulations (like COPPA) based on user age profiles. 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page. 9.
